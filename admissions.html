Overview of Admissions

“Above all, we are committed to student success. That commitment refers to more than graduation rates. It includes the transmission of ideals, such as ethics and civic responsibility. It incorporates our intention to prepare our students to thrive as global citizens. And, it reflects our intent to make each student’s college experience as fulfilling as possible.”

Dr. E. Joseph Savole, President

Prospective Students

There has never been a more exciting time to be at The University of Louisiana, Lafayette. Founded in 1901, this university has been transformed in recent years by new initiatives that have greatly enhanced the undergraduate experience. We hope that as you plan your future, you will take time to look closely at the special opportunities The University of Lafayette has to offer.

Transfer Students

Find your future at The University of Louisiana! Transfer students are an important facet of the UL community, contributions ranging from world-class research to top tier athletics.

Graduate Students

We manage the application process, monitor student progress, and ultimately approve students for graduation including the final review of theses and dissertations among many other services.

International Students

The University of Louisiana, Lafayette had 972 international students for the Fall 2011 semester. We welcome international students as we hope to unite students, peers, and faculty from around the world to promote cultural and scholastic excellence.

Deadlines and Enrollment

College admission requirements are changing in Fall 2012! If you are planning on attending college in 2012, make sure you are prepared by learning about our new admission requirements. Taking the ACT exam early and often will help prepare for the application process. Stay updated with deadlines for enrollment by using the academic planning calendar.

Financial Aid

Wondering what the cost of attendance will be for upcoming school year of Fall 2012? Our financial aid page provides you with the cost of tuition, types of financial aid available, and the deadline to submit your financial aid application.
