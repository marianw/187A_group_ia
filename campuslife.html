Overview of Campus Life

Your education at the University of Louisiana, Lafayette, is not just reserved for the classroom. We encourage you to take advantage of the many opportunities that you will encounter on our campus.

Activities

This campus is constantly buzzing with action. There are programs geared toward students on a daily basis and we strive to incorporate the need for a balance of academics and co-curricular activities.

Events

Join us as the University of Louisiana at Lafayette celebrates Black History Month. Check the calendar of events for more details and future events. With our many clubs and student organizations on campus there are always new events and opportunities.

Getting Involved

Take a closer look at what UL, Lafayette, has to offer in terms of student involvement. Be a part of the Student Government or even get started on finding internships and leadership positions on campus.

Athletics

The UL Ragin’ Cajuns are proud to be a part of Division 1 Sports. We pride ourselves on our dedication to excellence on and off the field. See what the UL, Lafayette Athletics are all about!

Clubs

Join one of the many clubs on campus. If you cannot find a club that suits you interests, start your own! The Greek life on campus brings together students of all disciplines and interests. There’s something to offer for everyone.
