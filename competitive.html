Addition of "Contact"

Reason for the addition of "Contact" Navigation

We decided to include a “Contact” heading on the main navigation to allow for quick access to basic information. This would help the user who is trying to find information in a hurry. Although the websites we used for the competitive analysis did not have a “Contact” heading we decided this was important for our redesign in order for our redesign to be informative and efficient.

Final Navigation Bar

Summary and Final Navigation Bar

For the main navigation we used our results from our competitive analysis and also used our user scenarios to help visualize the best way to navigate through a university website. We decided on breaking down the user types on the homepage to visually categorize the site users. We also decided that a dual navigation would be used, incorporating a main navigation bar with an additional side navigation bar. The original site had alot of navigation errors, we hoped to fix many of them with our new design incorporating both the user type and most popular heading titles. We included U-Link as that is the primary user portal for their current students and most current students would visit the site to exclusively go to U-Link.
